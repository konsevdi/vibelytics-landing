Privacy

Effective May 18, 2026. Vibelytics is a static pilot website for an AI launch copilot. This notice explains the limited information involved in using this site.

This static site does not include accounts, payment forms, analytics scripts, advertising pixels, backend APIs, or attendee monitoring features. If you email us at yes@vibelytics.ai, we receive the information you choose to send.

The pilot uses synthetic examples in your browser. Changing fields on the pilot page updates local static demo content. The site does not send those demo selections to a backend.

Your choices
